Who We Are

AnimeLuv is Jacksonville's first anime store! We are located in the Southside of Jacksonville inside the Avenues mall.


We specialize in all thing's anime. We sell figures, posters, body pillows, manga, and more! We would love to get your guy’s feedback on what to add to the store! Some of our favorite anime include Neon Genesis Evangelion, Attack on Titan, Jujutsu Kaisen, My hero Academia, Danganronpa, and the Rising of Shield Hero. We pride ourselves on having official merchandise at a great price. Additionally, our store holds special events throughout the month. We have cosplay and art contests and have trivia days.


Please stop by and say hi! Or even to just watch anime. We have anime projected on the walls!! In fact, come in cosplay and get 10% off. We can’t wait to see you.
