Krista LeRoy

Hello, my name is Krista LeRoy, and I am 21 years old. I am working towards getting my bachelor's degree in Information Technology Management at Florida State College at Jacksonville. I switched my major a couple of times in the process. Ultimately, it was not until I built my first gaming computer that I fell in love with technology. In my free time, I love to play video games, watch anime, and make baked goods.

Currently, I am enrolled in a web technology class that focuses on the fundamentals of HTML and CSS. This is my first time working with these languages, and at times it can be challenging. However, after each assignment is done, it is rewarding to see the websites created. I love that I can build something and immediately see the result. Additionally, I love the creative aspect of web development.

I am excited to see where this class takes me! So far, we have primarily focused on the basic structure of HTML and CSS. Additionally, we began learning the basics of designing a page layout: creating floating elements, grid-based layouts, positioning styles, etc. I am most excited to learn how to integrate e-commerce into websites.
